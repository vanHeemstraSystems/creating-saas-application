Visualizing large hierarchies

You can use FoamTree version 3.2.0 or later to browse deeply-nested hierarchies containing 100k+ groups at 100+ levels. To make this possible, FoamTree defers computation and rendering of diagrams on lower levels of the hierarchy until the corresponding groups get open and zoomed enough to be clearly visible.

This demo visualizes Tree of Life datasets available from www.phyloxml.org.

Use the search box or links below to zoom to a specific node. Double click a group to open it and zoom. Press Esc to zoom out.
